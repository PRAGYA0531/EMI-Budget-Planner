Problem Statement
• Users often struggle to calculate loan EMIs accurately and compare different repayment scenarios. A simple, clear, and reliable tool is needed to help them plan finances better. It lso tells us how much loan and EMI we can afford depending on our expenses and income.

Scope of the Project
• The project focuses on calculating EMI, total interest, and total payment based on user inputs like loan amount, interest rate, and tenure and income. It includes a GUI for easy usage and supports basic financial planning and balancing.

Target Users
• Anyone taking a loan such as students, working professionals, and individuals who want quick EMI estimations without complex financial knowledge and much struggle and is easy to understand.

High-Level Features
• Easy to use graphical interface.
• Accurate EMI, total interest, and total payment calculation.
• Instant results based on user input.
• Clear display of financial breakdown.
